Title: Email Campaigns for Summer Sales

Body:
Email is a powerful channel for summer sale ad copy. Personalize subject lines and body content to drive opens and clicks. Focus on exclusive offers to make subscribers feel special.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Exclusive Summer Sale: 40% Off Just for You!”
- “Your Summer Deal Awaits – Open for 50% Off!”

Tips:
1. Use personalized subject lines (e.g., “John, Your Summer Deal Awaits!”).
2. Include a clear CTA button like “Shop Now.”

Segment your email list for better targeting and higher conversions.
