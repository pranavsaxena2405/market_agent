Title: Incorporating Testimonials in Summer Sale Ads

Body:
Customer testimonials build trust in summer sale ad copy. Use real quotes to show product value and encourage purchases.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Loved My Summer Gear! Save 30% Now!” – Sarah K.
- “Best Summer Deals Ever! Shop 40% Off!” – Mike T.

Tips:
1. Use short, authentic testimonials.
2. Pair quotes with specific products.

Test testimonials in email and social media ads.
