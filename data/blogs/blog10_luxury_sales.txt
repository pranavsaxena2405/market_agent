Title: Ad Copy for Luxury Summer Sales

Body:
Luxury summer sales require sophisticated ad copy that emphasizes exclusivity and quality. Highlight premium products and limited availability to appeal to high-end shoppers.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Elevate Your Summer with Exclusive 20% Off Luxury!”
- “Timeless Summer Style – Save on Premium Collections!”

Tips:
1. Use elegant language like “curated” or “bespoke.”
2. Focus on quality over discounts.

Test luxury copy on affluent audiences via email or social media.
