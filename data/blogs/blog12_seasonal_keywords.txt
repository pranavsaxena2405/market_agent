Title: Using Seasonal Keywords in Summer Sale Ad Copy

Body:
Seasonal keywords like “sun,” “beach,” or “summer” make ad copy feel timely and relevant. Incorporate them to evoke the season and attract attention.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Sun-Kissed Savings! 40% Off Summer Essentials!”
- “Beach-Ready Deals – Shop Now and Save!”

Tips:
1. Use keywords that match your audience’s summer activities.
2. Avoid overusing keywords to maintain authenticity.

Test keyword-rich copy in SEO-driven campaigns.
