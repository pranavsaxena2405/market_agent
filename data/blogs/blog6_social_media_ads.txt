Title: Summer Sale Ad Copy for Social Media

Body:
Social media is perfect for summer sale ads due to its visual nature and wide reach. Craft short, punchy copy that pairs with vibrant images to grab attention on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Summer Vibes Only! 30% Off Your Favorites – Shop Now!”
- “Sunshine and Savings! Grab Summer Deals on Instagram!”

Tips:
1. Use emojis (e.g., ☀️) to enhance visual appeal.
2. Include hashtags like #SummerSale to boost discoverability.

Track engagement metrics to refine social media ad copy.
