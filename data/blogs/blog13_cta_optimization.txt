Title: Optimizing Calls to Action in Summer Sale Ads

Body:
A strong call to action (CTA) turns interest into action. Summer sale ad copy needs clear, compelling CTAs to drive clicks and sales.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Shop Now for 50% Off Summer Must-Haves!”
- “Grab Your Summer Deals – Click Here!”

Tips:
1. Use action verbs like “shop,” “grab,” or “discover.”
2. Keep CTAs short and urgent.

Test different CTAs to find the most effective ones.
