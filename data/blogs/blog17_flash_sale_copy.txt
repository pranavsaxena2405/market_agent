Title: Writing Ad Copy for Summer Flash Sales

Body:
Flash sales are short and intense, requiring ad copy that grabs attention fast. Emphasize limited time and big savings to drive quick action.

Example Ad Copy:
- “24-Hour Summer Flash Sale! 50% Off Everything!”
- “Flash Deal: Save Big Before Midnight!”

Tips:
1. Use timers or countdowns in copy.
2. Keep messaging ultra-concise.

Test flash sale copy in social media stories.
