Title: Using FOMO in Summer Sale Ad Copy

Body:
Fear of missing out (FOMO) is a powerful motivator. Summer sale ad copy that emphasizes limited-time offers or exclusive deals can drive quick purchases.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Don’t Miss Out! Summer Sale Ends Tonight!”
- “Exclusive Summer Deals – Grab Yours Before They’re Gone!”

Tips:
1. Use phrases like “Only a Few Left!” to create scarcity.
2. Highlight exclusivity to make customers feel special.

Test FOMO-driven copy in flash sales for maximum impact.
