Title: Personalized Summer Sale Ad Copy

Body:
Personalized ad copy makes customers feel valued. Use data like names or past purchases to tailor summer sale messaging for higher engagement.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Jane, Your Summer Favorites Are 30% Off!”
- “Exclusive Summer Deal for You: Save 40%!”

Tips:
1. Use CRM data for personalization.
2. Test dynamic content in emails.

Personalized copy boosts conversions in targeted campaigns.
