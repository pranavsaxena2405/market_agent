Title: Cross-Channel Consistency in Summer Sale Ads

Body:
Consistent ad copy across channels builds brand trust. Ensure your summer sale messaging aligns on social media, email, and websites.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Summer Savings Everywhere! 40% Off Online & In-Store!”
- “Same Great Summer Deals Across All Platforms!”

Tips:
1. Use consistent slogans across channels.
2. Adapt tone slightly for each platform.

Track cross-channel performance to ensure coherence.
