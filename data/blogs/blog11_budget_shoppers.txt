Title: Targeting Budget Shoppers in Summer Sales

Body:
Budget-conscious shoppers love summer sales for great deals. Craft ad copy that emphasizes value and affordability without sacrificing quality.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Summer Savings for All! Up to 70% Off!”
- “Affordable Summer Fun – Shop Deals Under $20!”

Tips:
1. Highlight low prices or bundle deals.
2. Use relatable language to connect with budget shoppers.

Test budget-friendly copy in mass-market campaigns.
