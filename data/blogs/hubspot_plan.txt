A marketing plan aligns your advertising goals with audience insights and budget allocation. Here's a 6-step framework:

1. Define your advertising goals clearly (e.g., increase sales, boost traffic).
2. Identify your audience personas and platforms they use.
3. Choose your advertising channels (Google, Meta, LinkedIn).
4. Set your budget across channels and campaign types.
5. Develop and schedule your ads.
6. Measure performance using analytics tools.

Ad planning is iterative. Make data-driven tweaks regularly for best results.
