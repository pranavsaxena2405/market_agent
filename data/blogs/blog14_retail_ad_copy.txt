Title: Retail-Focused Summer Sale Ad Copy

Body:
Retail summer sales thrive on in-store and online excitement. Craft ad copy that drives foot traffic and online purchases with clear, enticing messaging.

Example Ad Copy:
- “Summer Sale in Stores Now! Save Up to 50%!”
- “Shop Online or In-Store for Summer Deals!”

Tips:
1. Highlight both online and in-store availability.
2. Use location-specific copy for local stores.

Test retail copy in flyers and online ads.
