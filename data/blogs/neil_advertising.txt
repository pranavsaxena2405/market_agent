Online advertising is a powerful tool for reaching your target audience at scale. It allows businesses to drive traffic, build brand awareness, and generate leads. The key platforms include Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter.

Best practices:
1. Define your customer persona and campaign goals.
2. Use compelling headlines and high-quality creatives.
3. Test different ad formats (carousel, video, lead gen forms).
4. Track KPIs such as CTR, conversion rate, and ROAS.

Remember, online advertising is about experimenting, optimizing, and doubling down on what works.
